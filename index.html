Everyday email

The rest of email matters too.

Cove Mail includes the everyday tools you need to keep email moving.

01Write from the right account

Use the right sender and signature for each account, with support for drafts, replies, forwards, and attachments.

02Reliable sending

Track queued and failed messages in an Outbox instead of wondering what happened.

03Know when something needs attention

See sync progress and find account problems before they get in your way.

04Keep mail available offline

Choose which mailboxes and attachments stay available without a connection.

05Rules you can understand

Put mail rules in the order you want, see what they did, and review activity across accounts.
